Sunflower Plant Care Guide

Soil for Sunflowers

Well-drained, fertile soil.

Sunflowers thrive in well-drained and fertile soil. Ensure good nutrient levels for optimal growth and bloom.

Watering

Regular watering, especially during dry periods.

Provide consistent moisture to sunflowers, especially during dry periods, to support healthy flowering.

Sunlight

Full sun exposure.

Plant sunflowers in an area with full sunlight exposure for optimal photosynthesis and vibrant blooms.

Benefits

Beautiful and cheerful blooms.

Sunflowers produce large and vibrant blooms, adding beauty and cheerfulness to your garden.

Attracts pollinators.

Sunflowers attract pollinators like bees, enhancing biodiversity in your garden.

Considerations

Support tall varieties; watch for pests such as aphids; deadhead for prolonged blooming.
